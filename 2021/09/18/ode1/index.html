$h=-(vt-\frac{1}{2}at^2)=25\,\mathrm{m}$.

2.

Let $c$ denotes the pollutant concentration.

$\begin{cases}\frac{\mathrm{d}c}{\mathrm{d}t}\times10000\,\mathrm{m^3}=50\,\mathrm{m^3/day}\times\frac{m_0}{5}\,\mathrm{g/m^3}-c\times200\,\mathrm{m^3/day}\\c(0)=5m_0\,\mathrm{g/m^3}\\\end{cases}$

Solve this ode, we get

Now let $c(t)=m_0\,\mathrm{g/m^3} $, we can work out that $t=50\ln\frac{99}{19}\,\mathrm{day}$.

3.

Repeated infection able case.

$\begin{cases}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t}=km-\mu x\\x(0)=x_0\end{cases}$

Repeated infection unable case.

$\begin{cases}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t}=k(m-y)-\mu x\\\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t}=\mu x\\x(0)=x_0\\y(0)=0\end{cases}$

4.

$\begin{cases}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}t}=2\\\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}t}=\frac{x(100-x)}{1000}\\x(0)=0\\y(0)=0\end{cases}$

solve this ode, we get

$\begin{cases}x=2t\\y=\frac{-t^3+75t^2}{750}\end{cases},0\le t\le 50$.

5.

The objects are under buoyant, gravity and viscous resistance’s effect.

$\begin{cases}\frac{\mathrm{d}v}{\mathrm{d}t}=g-\frac{\rho_{\text{water}} gV+\lambda v}{m}\\v(0)=0\end{cases}$

Solve this ode, we get $v(t)=\mathrm{e}^{\int_0^t\frac{-\lambda}{m}\,\mathrm{d}s}\int_0^t(1-\frac{\rho_{\text{water}}V}{m})g\cdot\mathrm{e}^{\int_0^s\frac{\lambda}{m}\,\mathrm{d}r}\,\mathrm{d}s=(1-\frac{\rho_{\text{water}}V}{m})(1-\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{m}})\frac{mg}{\lambda}$.

So $h(t)=(1-\frac{\rho_{\text{water}}V}{m})(t+\frac{m}{\lambda}\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{m}}-\frac{m}{\lambda})\frac{mg}{\lambda}$.

When $h_{\text{sphere}}>h_{\text{cylinder}}$, we have $(m_{\text{s}}-\rho_{\text{water}}V_{\text{s}})(t+\frac{m_{\text{s}}}{\lambda}\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{m_{\text{s}}}}-\frac{m_{\text{s}}}{\lambda})>(m_{\text{c}}-\rho_{\text{water}}V_{\text{c}})(t+\frac{m_{\text{c}}}{\lambda}\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{m_{\text{c}}}}-\frac{m_{\text{c}}}{\lambda})$,

i. e. $(1-\frac{4-\pi}{5-\frac{4}{3}\pi})t<\frac{1000[(5\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{5000}}-5)-\frac{4-\pi}{5-\frac{4}{3}\pi}(4\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{4000}}-4)]}{\lambda}$.

At moment $t=0$ both sides is $0$. Calculate the derivatives on both sides, we have $1-\frac{4-\pi}{5-\frac{4}{3}\pi}>-\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{5000}}+\frac{4-\pi}{5-\frac{4}{3}\pi}\mathrm{e}^{\frac{-\lambda t}{4000}}$ holds for all $t>0$.

Thus the inequality above is impossible, which means $h_{\text{sphere}}0$, i. e. the cylinder will reach the bottom first whatever the depth of water is.

6.

(1)

The tangent line of point $(x_0,y_0)$ is $y-y_0=y’(x_0)(x-x_0)$.

$\frac{1}{2}|y_0-y’(x_0)x_0||\frac{y’(x_0)x_0-y_0}{y’(x_0)}|=a^2$

This equation holds for all $(x_0,y_0)$. Replace $(x_0,y_0)$ by $(x,y)$ we get an ode

$2a^2|y’|=y^2-2xyy’+x^2(y’)^2$

(2)

$\sqrt{(y_0-y’(x_0)x_0)^2+(\frac{y’(x_0)x_0-y_0}{y’(x_0)})^2}=l$

This equation holds for all $(x_0,y_0)$. Replace $(x_0,y_0)$ by $(x,y)$ we get an ode

$(y’)^2l^2=(y^2-2xyy’+x^2(y’)^2)(1+(y’)^2)$

3.

(1)

$(\frac{y-x^2}{x})’=0$

Induces the ode

$xy’-x^2-y=0$.

(2)

$xy’+y=0$

(3)

The set of all circle in the plane is $\{(x-a)^2+(y-b)^2=r^2\}$ with three arbitrary constant $a,b,r$.

Firstly, eliminate $r$.

$2x-2a+2yy’-2by’=0$

Then eliminate $a$.

$1+yy’’+(y’)^2-by’’=0$

Finally eliminate $b$.

$(\frac{1+yy’’+(y’)^2}{y’’})’=0$

Induces the ode

$(yy’’’+3y’y’’)y’’-(1+yy’’+(y’)^2)y’’’=0$

i. e. $3y’(y’’)^2-y’’’-(y’)^2y’’’=0$.

It is actually an order-$2$ ode.

(4)

$(\frac{2y}{9+x^2})’=0$

Induces the ode

$(9+x^2)y’-2xy=0$.
